## Draft
### Lesson of the Week
The past week has been a lesson in faith…

To not see the way forward and to only have the vision of lays in front of you. 

This has been an extremely frustrating experience for me. As I progress forward there is always a comfort in knowing what it is next. 

Though, with my refreshed sense of self and better understanding of my conative, I moving in ways that have been foreign to me for some time. 

Natural, yet foreign. 

It has been a gruelling time of sacrifice. While fast this Ramadan I have been pushing myself shed many part of me that no longer serve my present, nor will they my future. 

Far too often we drag along people, things, beliefs, and habits that weigh us down. 

Drop the dead weight. 

Drop it all. 

All at once or piece by piece, the weight MUST be dropped. 

This is how we best move forward.

As clear space in our lives, we make room for new. 

Embrace the new. 

Stay out of the prison of your mind. 
—
Sincerely,
Cyrus

### Act of Self-Love
This week I would like to encourage you to investigate the baggage you are still carrying around. Can you drop one or two this week?
