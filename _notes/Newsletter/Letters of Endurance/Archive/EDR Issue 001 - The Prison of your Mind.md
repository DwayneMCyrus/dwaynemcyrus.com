## Bullet Draft
Lesson learned: Your mind is your prison and your freedom
Perspective shift: Your environment does not need to dictate your behaviour
Activity: In writing by hand, explore what emotional burdens you are carry and using to keep yourself in a position that doesn’t serve you.

## Template Draft
As survivors our mind can easily and often becomes our jailer. Many of us have been living in a cell of our own creation for years. 

I am no different. 

My mind has become my cage for many years and it has dictated my actions in the world. It has been a horrific because I have become increasingly hyper-conscience of the experience.

Having taken on the life of a “lone-wolf”, I have made the emotional burdens of others close to me my mine to bear. These burdens have become the chains and bars in which I have used to create my prison of my mind. 

Now here’s are hard truth I had to accept this past week. 

You do not need anyone’s permission or acknowledgment to drop all of their emotional burdens.

Permission was asked of me to take on the emotional weight of my mother/father and their decisions in life. So why in the world have I been telling myself I need their permission or acknowledgment to now reject it.

Think about it. 

You have the key to the life you want to live and it begins in your mind. 

This key is accessible to everyone and anyone.

I did not get here on my own I had a few brothers, beautiful men who I have recently brought into my circle reinforce this truth. 

This is the start of our voyage together. 

Stop asking others for permission or justification to be who you need to be for you. This what I am doing in this very moment and it is not easy, but it is necessary to your survival.

The temptation to go back into that prison of my mind is extreme compelling.

This is the work. 

This is your start.

This is your voyage.

The moment you **decide and commit** that you will only act in **your best interest** is the moment that you have **stepped out of your prison**.

Your mind is greater than your environment. 

Never allow your environment to dictate your personal power. 

With Love, 
D Cyrus
